Accommodation

Explore our accommodation options

  • Accommodation Options
  • Virtual Tour
  • Student Life
  • Fees & Finance

We offer a variety of options and locations to find the space that works for you, with the majority of our residences being only a short walk from campus.

Living in university accommodation is the best way to get the most out of your university experience, a chance to make new friends while getting used to living away from home. 

Why live with us?  

Guaranteed accommodation 

We guarantee University-managed accommodation for all first-year students*.  

Free Sports Centre Membership  

As a resident in our University-managed accommodation, you will receive free Platinum gym membership to the University’s Sports Centre, giving you access to all of the facilities, and fitness and wellness classes.  

Wide range of accommodation options  

Each residence has its own distinctive character, offering you a safe and secure environment in which to socialise, study and relax. Our accommodation options include studios, self-catered or catered halls, and en-suite rooms or flats with shared facilities.
We also have dedicated accommodation for students who are Welsh speakers or learners. We offer a range of licence lengths to suit different courses, and all of our accommodation is within walking distance to academic and campus facilities.  

Hassle-free living  

While living in our accommodation, there is no need to worry about fluctuating bills as the accommodation fees include utilities, wifi, and a high level of personal contents insurance. Our residents have access to 24/7 help and assistance, including a staffed reception. Located near to our accommodation options are our 24/7 learning centers, which are open spaces dedicated for group work studying. As soon as you arrive, our Residences team will welcome you into your new home and our campus community.  

Good quality accommodation  

We are proud to have been placed Top in the UK  for Halls and Student Accommodation in the WhatUni? Student Choice Awards 2022. Our Accommodation conforms to the Universities UK Code of Practice for University Managed Student Accommodation. The University subscribes to the Universities UK Student Accommodation Code which has been established to protect your rights to safe, good-quality accommodation, wherever you are studying and to ensure that you get the best out of your time living in our accommodation. 

*If you apply for accommodation by 1 September 2022.  

Information for Undergraduate Applicants

If you have received a conditional or unconditional offer to study at Aberystwyth University and are holding us as either your firm or insurance choice, we will send you an invitation to apply for accommodation, both by email and post, from April in your year of entry. We advise that you complete your application as soon as possible, as we offer accommodation on a first-come, first-served basis. We can also continue to provide you with accommodation throughout your time here. If you would like to live with the friends you have made in your first year, then you can apply to live together. Please note that the listed accommodation options are subject to change for future academic years; further information and updates are available at www.aber.ac.uk/accommodation

 

Information for Postgraduate Applicants

Please note: we recognise that some postgraduate courses run for 52 weeks and so we offer a variety of licence lengths to suit you, but please be aware that the longest length of stay we can offer is 50 weeks.

*To qualify for a guaranteed place applicants must ensure that their accommodation application is received by the 1st September in the year of entry and that they respond to the offer of accommodation by the deadline date detailed in the Accommodation Offer. Please note that a place in university-owned or managed accommodation is guaranteed, but not a specific type of room or location.

Please see our Priorities Policy for additional information.
